# Rewards, Promotions, and Legal Plan

**Current state:** subscription raffle and monthly bonus fragments are production-blocked. Legal pages exist; versioned acceptance is undeployed.

## Approved direction pending counsel

- Remove subscription-linked raffle eligibility and synthetic per-subscriber pool.
- Keep any social giveaway separate from paid access with no purchase necessary.
- Instagram/social distribution does not remove GRIT's sponsor responsibility.
- Do not enable cash or chance-based rewards before jurisdiction-specific review.

## Decisions required

- Whether rewards exist at launch at all.
- Eligible jurisdiction, age, prize type/value, entry method, and frequency.
- Official rules, tax/reporting, identity, sanctions, and winner-publicity process.
- Skill competition versus random sweepstakes; do not mix without counsel.

## Delivery phases

1. Disable/remove subscription raffle code, dashboard state, rules, and marketing claims.
2. Deploy versioned legal acceptance for actual enabled behavior.
3. If approved, create a separate promotion service with campaign/rules version, free entry, immutable entrant list, eligibility checks, auditable draw, winner/alternate workflow, and privacy retention.
4. Use a funded prize budget/treasury record independent from subscriptions.
5. Add approval, identity/tax collection, payout provider, failure/retry, evidence, and reconciliation.
6. Publish platform-required disclaimers and official rules for each campaign.

## Proof

No-purchase entry; duplicate/fraud controls; start/end timezone; ineligible geography/age; auditable deterministic draw record; winner timeout/alternate; payout fail/retry; tax threshold; deletion/retention; rules-version evidence.

## Definition of done

Counsel-approved rules match code; payment never changes odds; every entrant/draw/payout is auditable; prize funding and payout reconcile; disabled campaigns expose no entry or claims.

**Complexity:** L-XL depending scope. **Production risk:** Very high.
